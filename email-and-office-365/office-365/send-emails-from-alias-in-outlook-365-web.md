# Send emails from alias in Outlook 365 Web

## Steps

1. Open the [**Exchange Admin Center**](https://admin.exchange.microsoft.com/)
2. Click **Settings**> **Mail flow**
3. Enable **Turn on sending from aliases** &#x20;
