# How to Add Domain and Create New Email Account in MailEnable

### Steps to Add Domain and Email <a href="#steps-to-add-domain-and-email" id="steps-to-add-domain-and-email"></a>

#### Step 1: Open Messaging Manager <a href="#step-1-open-messaging-manager" id="step-1-open-messaging-manager"></a>

To start, log in to your MailEnable server.

* Locate the Messaging Manager in the left-hand navigation panel.
*   Right-click on the Messaging Manager to open the available options.<br>

    <figure><img src="https://images.cloudclusters.io/31e3ced6126e4c598e97b519a25f38e7/right-click-on-the-messaging-manager.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

\
This is the starting point for adding a new PostOffice (mail domain).

#### Step 2: Create a New PostOffice (Mail Domain) <a href="#step-2-create-a-new-postoffice-mail-domain" id="step-2-create-a-new-postoffice-mail-domain"></a>

* Select **Create PostOffice** from the right-click menu.
*   A dialog box will appear asking you to enter a **PostOffice Name**.

    *   Example: If your domain is `example.com`, you can enter `example`.<br>

        <figure><img src="https://images.cloudclusters.io/7baf03d9a48d46a09885469bfd200f10/select-create-postoffice.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>


* Click **OK** to confirm.

This step is important because the PostOffice is essentially the **email domain** where your new email accounts will be created.

#### Step 3: Add a New Domain to the PostOffice <a href="#step-3-add-a-new-domain-to-the-postoffice" id="step-3-add-a-new-domain-to-the-postoffice"></a>

* After creating the PostOffice, **right-click** on it.
*   Select **New Domain**.<br>

    <figure><img src="https://images.cloudclusters.io/248a98b85f2f4dd887b7227947381871/new-domain-under-the-postoffice.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



* Enter the **full domain name** (e.g., `example.com`).
* Click **OK**.

Now, your email domain is successfully added and ready to host mailboxes.

#### Step 4: Create a New Mailbox (Email Account) <a href="#step-4-create-a-new-mailbox-email-account" id="step-4-create-a-new-mailbox-email-account"></a>

To **make a new email account**, follow these steps:

* Select the domain you just created.
* Right-click on **Mailboxes** under the domain.
*   Click **New Mailbox**.<br>

    <figure><img src="https://images.cloudclusters.io/0cf49fc82aab4837ac714f3af00aa440/select-the-domain.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



#### Step 5: Enter Email Account Information <a href="#step-5-enter-email-account-information" id="step-5-enter-email-account-information"></a>

In the new mailbox dialog, enter the following:

* **Mailbox Name**: This will be the username before the “@” (e.g., `john`).
* **Password**: Enter a secure password for the mailbox.
*   **Mailbox Type**: Choose the type of mailbox you need (e.g., Standard Mailbox).<br>

    <figure><img src="https://images.cloudclusters.io/02ae25578bd749d9ba61e712567c2789/enter-the-email-account-information.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

\
Click **OK** to save the new mailbox.

#### Step 6: Verify the New Email Account <a href="#step-6-verify-the-new-email-account" id="step-6-verify-the-new-email-account"></a>

To confirm the setup:

* Try logging in with the newly created email address (e.g., `john@example.com`).
* Use the password you assigned in the previous step.

If login is successful, your new email account is ready to use.
